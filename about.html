About FIBR

A small brand with a clear point of view.

Our Philosophy

FIBR was founded on a simple idea: clothing should help you think less, not more. We design pieces that work together — the same coat over a slip dress on Tuesday, over trousers on Friday. Less decision fatigue. More presence.

Every item is considered from material to silhouette. We favour natural fabrics, quiet colours, and cuts that hold their shape without demanding attention. If you've never noticed what someone was wearing but you never forgot how they looked — that's the FIBR effect.

"Buy less, choose well, make it last." — Vivienne Westwood

The Team

  • Léa M. — Creative Direction & Styling

Our Process

  1. Research the season's silhouette language
  2. Source sustainable fabrics from European mills
  3. Prototype three versions of each garment
  4. Live-test each piece for at least two weeks
  5. Photograph in natural light, no retouching
  6. Release in small batches — never overstock
