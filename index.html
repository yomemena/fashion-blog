How to Build a Look

The Formula

  1. Start with a base layer — a simple tee, tank, or slip dress
  2. Add a structure piece — blazer, coat, or oversized shirt
  3. Define the silhouette with a belt or tuck
  4. Choose one statement — bag, shoe, or accessory
  5. Edit until nothing is left to remove

Key Pieces This Season

  • Oversized pinstripe blazer
  • Ivory slip midi dress
  • Wide-leg wool trousers
  • Leather tie belt (the detail that changes everything)
  • Structured tote bag
  • Pointed-toe flats or loafers
